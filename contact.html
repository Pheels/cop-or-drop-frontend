Frequently Asked Questions
What will happen if the timer runs out on a competition before all tickets are sold?
If the competition has not sold out all tickets before completion, the timer will add additional time in order for all the tickets to sell on the competition. This can only happen a maximum of 3 times. If then the competition has still not sold out a cash alternative will be awarded instead to the people who have entered.
If I win a competition how long until I receive my item?
Once the winner has been chosen the prize will be sent to the addressed winner 1st class signed for delivery the next working business day.
How do i know the winner of the competition is not fixed?
Cop or Drop guarantee that the winner is chosen randomly by hosting live draws for each competition and using our ball randomizer that our host will operate.
How many times can i enter a competition?
Every competition has a maximum entry level of 5 times per person.
