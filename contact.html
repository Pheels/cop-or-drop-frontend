Frequently Asked Questions
What will happen if the timer runs out on a competition before all tickets are sold?
  • On the occasion that all tickets are not sold we will add an additional time of 7 days to the timer.
  • If in the additional 7 days extra we have not sold all tickets, we will add an extra 72 hours.
  • If within those 72 hours extra added time we have not sold all tickets a cash alternative competition will take place instead, offering all competition applicants a chance to win

If I win a competition how long until I receive my item?
  • Once a winner has been chosen the winner will receive an email confirming their win from a member of our team.
  • Once we have a confirmed address we will send a member of the team to deliver the winning item within 3 - 5 business days.

How do i know the winner of the competition is not fixed?
  • Cop or Drop guarantee that the winner of every competition is chosen randomly by hosting live draws for each competition and using our ball randomizer that our host will operate.

How many times can i enter a competition?
  • Every competition has a maximum entry of 5 times per personnel account
